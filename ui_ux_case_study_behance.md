# Partnest: Bridging the Gap Between Investors and SMEs through Trust and Data

**Platform:** Mobile App (iOS / Android)  
**Role:** Lead Product Designer  
**Tools:** Figma, Illustrator, After Effects  
**Timeline:** 12 Weeks  

---

## 🚀 Overview

Partnest is a revolutionary mobile application designed to bridge the gap between Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) and potential investors. By centralizing vital business data and translating it into a standardized "Credibility Score," Partnest empowers investors to make confident, data-driven decisions while giving SMEs a fair platform to showcase their potential.

## ⚠️ The Problem

The SME investment landscape is plagued by a massive trust deficit. Investors struggle to verify the legitimacy, financial health, and potential of SMEs due to fragmented data and unstandardized reporting. On the other side, promising SME founders spend excessive time compiling evidence rather than building their businesses.

## 💡 The Solution

A dual-sided ecosystem wrapped in a premium, trustworthy mobile experience. Partnest provides:
1. **For Investors:** A curated discovery feed, deep-dive evidence rooms, and standardized credibility scoring.
2. **For SMEs:** An intuitive onboarding process to connect APIs and financial data to automatically generate an investor-ready profile.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

- **Angel Investors & Micro-VCs:** Looking for vetted, high-potential SMEs to invest in with minimal due-diligence friction.
- **SME Founders:** Seeking capital, needing a platform to organize and showcase their financial and operational metrics securely.

---

## 🎨 Design Process & System

### 1. Visual Identity
To inspire trust and professionalism while remaining modern and accessible.
- **Typography:** We used a clean, geometric sans-serif (Inter/SF Pro) to ensure legibility across dense financial data tables and dashboards.
- **Color Palette:** 
  - *Primary:* Deep Navy/Trust Blue (evoking security and finance)
  - *Secondary/Accents:* Vibrant Teal & Soft Gold (representing growth and premium quality)
  - *Status:* Semantic colors for Financial Metrics (Green for positive revenue trends, Red for trailing expense ratios).
  
### 2. The Design System
We built a robust component library tailored for iPhone 16 viewport dimensions to ensure crisp, scalable UI. From scalable `SmeBioContactCard` widgets to complex interactive charts for the Score Dashboard, every element was built as a reusable component in Figma.

---

## 📱 Key Screens & Interactions

### 1. The SME Discovery Feed
**The Goal:** Make discovering businesses feel as engaging as a high-end editorial feed.
**The Design:** Instead of a dry list, we designed cards featuring high-quality SME imagery, quick glance tags (Sector, Stage), and their top-level Credibility Score. The feed is highly filterable and dynamically sorts entities based on user preferences.

### 2. The Credibility Score Dashboard
**The Goal:** Demystify the score. 
**The Design:** A visually stunning dashboard where the Credibility Score is the hero element. Utilizing circular progress indicators and breakdown charts, we show *why* an SME got their score (e.g., Financial Metrics, Evidence completeness). We spent weeks refining the tooltip micro-interactions here.

### 3. Deep Dive Evidence Room
**The Goal:** Provide granular data without overwhelming the user.
**The Design:** A tabbed, accordion-style interface where investors can drill down into operational history, revenue trends, and expense ratios. Clean data visualization allows for quick consumption of complex historical timestamps.

### 4. Investor Comparison Watchlist
**The Goal:** Help investors compare side-by-side.
**The Design:** A horizontal scrolling matrix that aligns critical metrics—Credibility Score, MRR, Expense Ratio—across saved SMEs (e.g., comparing "Reelnosh" with other market players).

---

## ✨ Micro-interactions & Polish

A premium financial app lives on its details.
- **Score Animation:** When landing on a profile, the Credibility Score physically "counts up" to build anticipation.
- **Skeleton Loaders:** To mask backend fetching times, we utilized elegant skeleton loaders mirroring the exact UI geometry.
- **Haptic Feedback:** Subtle vibrations when adding an SME to the Comparison Watchlist or successfully submitting onboarding documents.

---

## 🏁 Conclusion & Learnings

Designing Partnest was an exercise in balancing dense financial data with a sleek, consumer-grade aesthetic. By prioritizing transparency and ease of data consumption through the Credibility Score Dashboard, we successfully created an environment where trust is built immediately.
